How to update to JEM 4 for Joomla 4 and PHP 8

  1. Update to JEM 2.3.6
  2. Make a backup!
  3. Disable the JEM extensions, all jem plugins, all JEM modules
  4. (During upgrade process, it suggested to switch to Joomla3 default template "Protostar")
  5. To prepare the update from Joomla 3 to Joomla 4, go to Components > Joomla! Update, choose Options and in Update Channel choose Joomla Next.
  6. Save.
  7. Then you get the informations about which other components are not ready. Perhaps you have to disable/uninstall other components/modules/plugins not prepared for Joomla 4
  8. Update to php 8.x
  9. Then upgrade from Joomla 3 to Joomla 4.
  10. Install JEM 4
  11. Before switching to the frontend, change the positions of the JEM-modules and the main menue, to the positions of your new Joomla 4 template (e.g. the Joomla 4 default template "cassiopeia"!
  12. Enable all JEM 4 extension (component, modules, plugins)
  13. Adapt the JEM settings (there are changes: e.g. date and time format)
  14. Check if everything is functioning correctly!
  15. Finish :-)
